Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

Newborn Software builds apps that respect your privacy. This policy explains what information our apps collect and how it’s used.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data (name, email, address) is collected by our apps.
  • No usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking SDKs are integrated.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers because we do not operate app-side servers.

App-Specific Behaviors (YatzyVibe)

  • Local storage: Game settings and statistics are stored on your device using iOS storage (e.g., UserDefaults).
  • Nearby play: To play with someone nearby, the app uses Apple frameworks (Local Network / Bluetooth) to connect devices peer-to-peer. Game state is synchronized directly between devices; no data passes through our servers.
  • Sounds & haptics: Audio and vibration settings are on-device only.

Permissions

  • Local Network: Required to discover and connect to nearby players on the same network.
  • Bluetooth: Used if Wi-Fi is unavailable for peer-to-peer connections.
  • Photos (optional): If you choose a custom background from Photos, read access is used to select an image. No images leave your device.
