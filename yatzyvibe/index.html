Highlights

  • Configurable scoring options and flexible straight rules
  • Haptic feedback and roll sounds (per-type toggles)

Quick Start

  1. Choose Play offline or Play nearby opponent.
  2. Roll up to three times, tap dice to hold, then pick a score box.
  3. Use Undo briefly after scoring if needed.
  4. Finish all categories to see the winner and stats.

Rules & Scoring

Upper section: Ones–Sixes (sum of matching dice). Reach 63+ for a Bonus (35 by default).

Lower section includes One Pair and Two Pair (Enhanced mode), Three/Four of a kind, Full House, Small/Large Straight, Yatzy, and Chance.

Many rules are configurable in Settings: Two-Pair format (distinct pairs vs any four-kind), small/large straight recognition, fixed-value or sum-based scores, bonus value, and more.

Settings

  • AI skill: Beginner / Normal / Expert
  • AI thinking time: 0.25 – 5.00s
  • Auto first roll: On / Off
  • Dice face: Pips / Numbers
  • Undo timer: 0–30s
  • Score highlights: Pending scores / Scores taken

Help

1. Rules of Play (Basics)

The goal is to achieve the highest score by rolling and selecting dice combinations. Players alternate turns; each turn allows up to three rolls before choosing a score.

2. Quick Start Guide / Game Flow
  • On first run, accept Local Network and Bluetooth prompts to enable nearby play.
  • Home: choose Play offline (Human or AI) or Play nearby opponent (Host/Join).
  • Each player gets 3 rolls per turn; tap dice to hold/unhold.
  • Use Undo within the countdown to revert the last score selection.
3. Tips & Strategy
  • Chase the 63-point upper-section pace for the bonus.
  • Preserve near-straights and high-value combos.
  • Use Chance for messy rolls.
4. Features
  • Offline vs Human/AI, Nearby via Wi-Fi/Bluetooth
  • Classic or Enhanced categories
  • Per-type haptics and sounds
  • Custom backgrounds and colors
  • Simple, readable score grid with pending/taken highlights
